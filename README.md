## 🧐 About

<img align="right" height="220" width="220" alt="" src="https://static.cdninstagram.com/rsrc.php/v3/yt/r/30PrGfR3xhB.png" style="border-radius: 20px; margin-left: 20px; border: 2px solid #4ac29a;"/>

Instagram Non-Followers Finder is a powerful userscript that helps you identify which Instagram accounts you follow that don't follow you back. It runs directly in your browser with a beautiful and user-friendly interface.

* 🔭 Accurately detects non-followers with Instagram's own API
* 🌱 Regular updates and new features
* 👯 Privacy-focused - all data stays in your browser
* 💬 Easy to use with intuitive controls
* 📫 Customizable settings for your specific needs

## 🛠️ How To Use

<div style="background-color: rgba(74, 194, 154, 0.1); border-radius: 10px; padding: 20px; margin: 20px 0; box-shadow: 0 4px 8px rgba(74, 194, 154, 0.2);">
  <ol>
    <li><b>Navigate to Instagram</b> - Open Instagram in your browser after installing the script</li>
    <li><b>Activate the Tool</b> - Click the eye icon (👁️) that appears in the bottom right corner</li>
    <li><b>Configure Settings</b> - Set your preferred options like scan limits and delay between requests</li>
    <li><b>Start Scanning</b> - Click "Find Non-Followers" to begin the process</li>
    <li><b>Review Results</b> - View the non-followers list and use the unfollow features as needed</li>
    <li><b>Save/Export</b> - Save your results or export them for later use</li>
  </ol>
</div>
